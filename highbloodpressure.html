High Blood Pressure


Yoga asanas involve breathing consciously and deeply while synchronizing your body movements. They can help control blood pressure naturally, primarily by relieving stress.Yoga asanas can soothe the nerves and help slow down an abnormal heart rate.


Here are some asanas on High Blood Pressure
1)Uttanasana (Standing forward bend pose)
2)Viparita Karani (Legs-up-the-wall pose)
3)Adho mukha svanasana (Downward-facing dog pose)
4)Pashchimottanasana (Seated Forward Bend Pose)
5)Setu Bandhasana (Bridge pose)
