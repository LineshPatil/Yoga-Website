Thyroid


There is a connection between stress and hypothyroidism, but certain yoga poses are thought to balance out thyroids that are either underactive or overactive . Several studies have shown the positive effect of yoga on improving thyroid function.


Here are some asanas on Thyroid
1)Setu Bandhasana (bridge pose)
2)Halasana (plough pose)
3)Matsyasana (fish pose)
4)Dhanurasana (bow pose)
5)Bhujangasana (cobra pose)
